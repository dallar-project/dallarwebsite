ABOUT DALLAR

The Dallar (DAL) is a fully functional blockchain to keep track of how many fucks you give. Unlike other cryptocurrencies, it was never intended to carry any real value. It is essentially a piece of group commentary that was meant to satirize the popular cryptocurrency trend, and is now a cultural experiment to Poe's Law.

To give a DAL is to give a fuck.

To fully understand how our blockchain works, we invite you to read our white paper.
