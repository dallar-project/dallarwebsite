About Dallar

The Dallar (DAL) is a fully functional blockchain to keep track of how many fucks you give. Unlike other cryptocurrencies, it was never intended to carry any real value. It is essentially a piece of group commentary that was meant to satirize the popular cryptocurrency trend, and is now a cultural experiment to Poe's Law.

To give a DAL is to give a fuck.

Our blockchain is essentially a fork of Groestlcoin, except our chain proof-of-work algorithm requires three rounds of Groestl hashing, an algorithm which we've named "throestl".

To fully understand our origin and purpose, we invite you to read our whitepaper.

Dallar Wallets

We currently have wallet clients for PC, OSX, and Android. iOS is coming soon.

Our desktop wallets are based on the original Bitcoin QT Core Wallet (forked from Groestlcoin's codebase). They serve as fully functional network nodes that help decentralize the blockchain.

Our available mobile wallets are based on BitPay's open-source mobile wallet Copay. While this wallet never releases your wallet's private keys, it still requires a connection to a web server to update and send transactions. A fully decentralized mobile wallet forked from BreadWallet is on our roadmap.



About The Logo

Our logo is the Korean word for "moon", which is spelled "dal" when typing Korean on an English keyboard. It is inscribed within a circle that symbolizes the moon.

We always try to use either a white or red Dallar logo, white being the moon's natural color as well as red being reminiscent of a blood moon. The red color is specifically #C31427, which is sampled directly from the NASA Graphics Standard Manual created in 1976.

Dallar is still very much a work in progress, including its branding, so these and all other details may change in the future.

Trading and Exchanging

Dallar's purpose isn't to maximize your profits through trading and exchanging, but again to give a fuck to our fellow man.

Dallar is a cryptocurrency however and it is destined to be exchanged in some form. Currently Dallar is only hosted on one exchange, but various community efforts to get listed on additional changes are underway. If you are aware of an exchange that might be interested in Dallar, please drop by our Discord and tell us about it.

Mining

Currently the easiest way to get some Dallar is to mine it. More ways to acquire Dallar are on our roadmap.

Mining Dallar essentially means turning your desktop's Graphics Card into a math cruncher, turning electrical power and Dallar transactions into mathematical guesses, hopefully which end up being a magical number that our blockchain will accept.

Currently we only offer two miners. More miners and an effort to simplify getting started with mining is on our roadmap.

One is a CUDA miner based on CCminer that requires Windows and relatively recent Nvidia GPU with up-to-date drivers.

The other is an AMD miner based on sgminer which requires Windows and an AMD GPU with up-to-date drivers.

For help with mining, please join our Discord for help.
